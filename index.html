Play live music videos anywhere

With Vidify you can reproduce in real time the videos playing on your computer on your computer, phone or TV.

  • Learn More

The perfect atmosphere

Vidify is ideal to play the right music videos on your TV, computer or even your phone. It's specially designed for bars, clubs and home theaters. Music videos are surprisingly entertaining: they help people get into the music and enjoy themselves more.

Fully automatic

Vidify reproduces the music videos automatically depending on what's playing on a computer. You do you, we'll take care of the videos.

Easy to use

All you have to do is open Vidify on a computer and, optionally, the app on your phone or television.

Cross platform

One of the most important parts is flexibility. You can reproduce the music videos on your phone, tablet, TV or computer with data from different players, like Spotify or VLC.

Customizable

Although Vidify offers sane defaults, it's up to you if you want lyrics, dark mode, a different quality playback...
